Chris Ward Lab's Reporting Automation Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Chris Ward Lab's Reporting Automation tool collects, uses, and shares information when the Reporting Automation tool (referred to as "the Service") is used, which utilizes the Google API to send automated emails on your behalf.

Information Collected

The following information may be collected when using the Service:

Information provided:

Your name and email address (for account setup and identification)

Recipient names and email addresses (for sending automated emails through your account)

Email content and metadata (subject lines, timestamps, attachments, etc.)

Other information provided when using the Service.

Information collected automatically through Google API:

The Google API is utilized to send emails from your designated Gmail account.

The Service will access the necessary data (your Gmail account credentials and ability to send emails) to perform this task.

The Service does not access, read, or modify any other Gmail content or data beyond sending the specific emails you authorize.

Limited data related to your use of the Google API, such as API calls and usage patterns, may be collected for internal monitoring and service improvement.

How Information is Used

The collected information is used to:

Provide the core functionality of the Service, including sending automated emails on your behalf.

Improve the Service and develop new features.

Communicate with you about the Service, updates, and support.

Ensure the security and stability of the Service and prevent fraud.

How Information is Shared

Personal information is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Non-personally identifiable information may be shared publicly and with partners (e.g., developers, service providers) for auditing and service maintenance purposes.

Google API and Data Privacy

The Google API Services User Data Policy is complied with, which governs how data from Google API Services is accessed and used. Only the minimum necessary permissions to provide the Service's functionality are requested. Restricted scopes data (email data) is used only for prominent, user-facing features and not for advertising purposes. You will be notified and your consent will be obtained before accessing or using any user data in ways not originally disclosed in this policy.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no system guarantees absolute security, and providing personal information is done at your own risk.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data.

You can manage third-party apps and sites that have access to your Google Account data.

User help documentation is provided, explaining how to manage and delete your data from the Service.

Updates to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. You will be notified of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website or within the Service.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact ward.chris.s@gmail.com.
